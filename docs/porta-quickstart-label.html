Porta Quick Start

First, charge: plug into USB-C for ~2-3 hours before first use. Then program your Porta in the browser (Chrome, Edge or Opera) with a data-capable USB-C cable.

  1. Connect

    Plug in via USB-C and open soniphorm.com/patcher. Click Connect and pick your Porta from the list.

  2. Check firmware

    Click the chip icon. If it says Update available, click Update -- no buttons to hold. If it can't connect: unplug & replug USB-C, power-cycle the Porta, then retry.

  3. Upload a patch

    Load an example or build your own, then click Upload (arrow icon). You'll hear it instantly. The patch stays on the device.
