Automation ​

Category: Control

Records and loops control movements on up to 8 channels. Long-press the encoder to record pot and button automation, then play it back in a loop.

Inputs ​

PortDescription
in_1Channel 1 input. Connect any control source to record.
in_2Channel 2.
in_3Channel 3.
in_4Channel 4.
in_5Channel 5.
in_6Channel 6.
in_7Channel 7.
in_8Channel 8.
record_triggerStart/stop recording. Connect to encoder or external gate.
mode_selectSelects record or playback mode.
clockExternal clock for tempo-synced playback.

Outputs ​

PortDescription
out_1Playback of channel 1.
out_2Channel 2.
out_3Channel 3.
out_4Channel 4.
out_5Channel 5.
out_6Channel 6.
out_7Channel 7.
out_8Channel 8.

Tips ​

Record a filter sweep: connect a Pot to in_1, long-press encoder, sweep the pot, stop. The out_1 replays your movement in a loop. Inputs expand dynamically as you connect them.
