Patcher

The Soniphorm Patcher is a visual patching environment for designing audio patches on Soniphorm hardware. Connect your device via USB, build a patch, and upload -- no coding required.

Launch Patcher

Requirements

  • Browser: Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge (Web Serial API required)
  • Connection: USB-C cable to your Soniphorm device
  • Supported hardware: Workshop, Smart Contact Mic, Eurorack

Safari and Firefox do not support Web Serial. Mobile browsers are not supported.
