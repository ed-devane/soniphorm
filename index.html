About Soniphorm

Soniphorm was established by Irish sound artist and educator Ed Devane in 2016. Based on the Wild Atlantic Way in Donegal, Ireland, every device is handcrafted — individually designed, fabricated, and tested.

Ed's artistic work explores sound, resonance, and vibration through custom-built instruments and electronics.
