Sound Craft Weekend Workshop

Gweedore, Co. Donegal — May Bank Holiday 2026

Saturday 2nd & Sunday 3rd May, 10am – 5pm

Soniphorm Sound Craft Studio, Gweedore Business Park, Derrybeg, Co. Donegal

Book Now

Two days in a working sound art studio on the Wild Atlantic Way. Make. Play. Perform.

This is a hands-on weekend workshop for anyone curious about sound, making, and what happens when the two collide. Over two days you'll explore a range of approaches to instrument building, sound design, and group improvisation using the tools, materials, and methods that drive the work here at Soniphorm. No experience necessary — just curiosity and a willingness to make noise.

We'll move through building, experimenting, listening, and playing together. Some things will work, some won't, but there's much to be learned in this.

Instruments want to be played, and playing to an audience justifies their existence. The course takes participants on a journey from exploration and experimentation with sound, through free play and improvisation, sound design using Soniphorm tools, instrument design and fabrication, and ultimately performance.

Sunday evening we take what we've made to Arcade Dungloe, a new artist-led space in the centre of town, for a short informal performance — a group improvisation presenting the results of the weekend. Solo sets welcome depending on who's up for it. This part is optional but strongly encouraged.

Getting here and making a weekend of it

Gweedore is worth the journey. Links to local affordable accommodation are available on request — or bring the campervan and take your pick of stunning spots in the area. Get the swims and hikes in around the course. The landscape here is part of the experience.

If you're travelling from Dublin, Galway, or further afield and want to share the drive, let us know where you're coming from when you sign up — we'll put you in touch with others making the same journey.

About the Studio

Sound Craft Studio is a dedicated workshop space in Gweedore, Co. Donegal — a place to explore sound, resonance, and creative electronics in a hands-on setting. Run by sound artist and educator Ed Devane, the studio offers workshops for all levels, from curious beginners to experienced practitioners looking to deepen their practice.

Introduction Workshops

Beginner-friendly half-day sessions. Build your own contact microphone, learn to record with it, and take home your creation. No experience necessary — just curiosity.

  • Solder and assemble a contact microphone
  • Learn recording techniques and mic placement
  • Guided listening walk around Gweedore
  • Take home your microphone and recordings

Weekend Intensives

Specialist two-day deep dives for those who want to go further. Build instruments, work with Soniphorm hardware and the Patcher software, and develop your creative practice.

  • Build and program a Smart Contact Microphone Kit
  • Work with exciters, resonance, and feedback systems
  • Field recording sessions on the Wild Atlantic Way
  • Take home your kit and all your work

Group & School Sessions

Tailored workshops for schools, community groups, arts organisations, and festivals. Hands-on creative sessions designed for any age group or skill level.

  • Customised to your group's needs and interests
  • Available at the studio or your venue
  • From single sessions to multi-week programmes
  • All materials and equipment provided

Location

Gweedore (Gaoth Dobhair), Co. Donegal, Ireland — on the Wild Atlantic Way. The studio is located in a Gaeltacht area surrounded by some of the most inspiring landscapes in Ireland.
