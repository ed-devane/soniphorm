Patcher

Visual patching environment for designing audio patches on Soniphorm hardware. Connect your device via USB, build a patch, and upload — no coding required.

  • Visual node-based patch editor
  • One-click upload to hardware via USB
  • Works with Smart Contact Mic Kit and Eurorack modules

Soundlab

Browser-based audio recorder, sample editor, sampler, and step sequencer. Record, slice, process, and sequence sounds — all from your phone or laptop, online or offline.

  • 16-slot sample grid with bank colour coding
  • Waveform editor with trim, fade, reverse, normalise
  • Record direct from your mic — no installs needed
  • Works offline as a PWA — install to your home screen
