Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Click the "Buy Now" button on the Products page to pay securely via Stripe. You'll be taken to a checkout page where you can enter your shipping details and pay by card. If you have any questions before ordering, DM me on Instagram @soniphorm.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit and debit card payments via Stripe. You can also DM me on Instagram @soniphorm to arrange alternative payment methods.

How much does shipping cost?

All orders ship from Donegal, Ireland via An Post. Shipping is added at checkout:

  • Ireland: €5 standard / €10 tracked
  • Europe: €10 standard / €15 tracked
  • Rest of World: €15 standard / €21 tracked

Tracked shipping includes tracking information and insurance.

What's the lead time for orders?

All products have a lead time of 1-2 weeks as each device is handcrafted to order. I'll confirm the exact timeline when you place your order.

What do I need to use the Active Exciter System?

You'll need:

  • Audio interface or mixer
  • 1/4" cables (for connecting your audio source)
  • USB charger (5V/1-2A, like a standard phone charger)
  • A pioneering spirit for sonic exploration!

Everything else (amplifier, exciters, cables, case) is included.

What is the Smart Contact Microphone Kit?

The Smart Contact Microphone Kit is a programmable contact microphone system you configure in the browser using the Soniphorm Patcher — no coding or Arduino installation required. Design your instrument's behaviour visually, flash it to the device in one click. The kit includes the main board, piezo, exciter, battery box, USB cable, and a breakout board with pots and buttons.

Priced at €120 for the kit, or €200 bundled with 3 × 1-hour mentoring sessions. Follow @soniphorm on Instagram or join the Discord for launch updates.

How do I get product support?

Join the Soniphorm Discord for product support, troubleshooting, and to connect with other users. You can also DM me on Instagram @soniphorm.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! I ship worldwide via An Post (Ireland's postal service). Shipping costs and times vary by destination — see the shipping cost breakdown above.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Each device is handcrafted and tested before shipping. If you receive a defective product or have concerns, please message me on Instagram @soniphorm or via the Discord and I'll work with you to resolve it.

Do you offer workshops or tutorials?

Yes! Sound Craft Studio in Gweedore, Donegal offers hands-on workshops from beginner sessions to specialist weekend intensives. See the Workshops page for details and booking. You can also check out tutorials and demos on YouTube.
